## CATEGORIA 1: FILOSOFIA FUNDAMENTAL SOBRE STARTUPS

### Definição e Propósito

> "A startup is a company designed to grow fast. Being newly founded does not in itself make a company a startup. Nor is it necessary for a startup to work on technology, or take venture funding, or have some sort of 'exit.' The only essential thing is growth."
**Contexto:** Essay "Startup = Growth" (2012)
**Significado:** Define startup por crescimento, não por idade ou tecnologia

> "The way to get startup ideas is not to try to think of startup ideas. It's to look for problems, preferably problems you have yourself."
**Contexto:** "How to Get Startup Ideas" (2012)
**Significado:** Ideias orgânicas > brainstorming forçado

> "Starting a startup is like jumping off a cliff and assembling a plane on the way down."
**Contexto:** Palestra em Stanford (2014)
**Significado:** Natureza caótica e urgente de startups

### Sobre Fundadores

> "What matters is not ideas, but the people who have them. Good people can fix bad ideas, but good ideas can't save bad people."
**Contexto:** "Ideas for Startups" (2005)
**Significado:** Founders > Ideas

> "The most important quality in a startup founder is determination. Not intelligence-- determination."
**Contexto:** "The Anatomy of Determination" (2009)
**Significado:** Grit supera talento

> "Startups don't win by attacking. They win by transcending. There are exceptions of course, but usually the way to win is to race ahead, not to stop and fight."
**Contexto:** "How to Start a Startup" (2005)
**Significado:** Foco em construir, não competir
