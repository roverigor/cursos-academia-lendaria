### 1. The "Who Else Wants" Formula
- **Template:** "Who Else Wants [Desirable Outcome]?"
- **Examples:**
  - "Who Else Wants To Be A Renegade Millionaire?"
  - "Who Else Wants A Flood Of New Customers?"
  - "Who Else Wants To Double Their Income In 90 Days?"

### 2. The "How To" Power Formula
- **Template:** "How To [Achieve Result] Without [Pain/Obstacle]"
- **Examples:**
  - "How To Get Rich In Mail Order Without Any Investment"
  - "How To Attract Customers Like A Magnet Without Cold Calling"
  - "How To Write Million Dollar Ads Without Being A Copywriter"

### 3. The "Secret/Discover" Hook
- **Template:** "The Secret [Authority] Use To [Achieve Outcome]"
- **Examples:**
  - "The Secret Millionaires Use To Multiply Money Fast"
  - "Discover The Hidden Marketing Tactics Of The Ultra-Rich"
  - "The Forbidden Selling Secret Banks Don't Want You To Know"

### 4. The "Warning" Interrupt
- **Template:** "Warning: [Threat] Unless You [Take Action]"
- **Examples:**
  - "Warning: Don't Spend Another Dollar On Advertising Until You Read This"
  - "Warning: Your Competition Is Using This Against You"
  - "Warning: 95% Of Businesses Fail Because They Don't Know This"

### 5. The "Announcing" News Formula
- **Template:** "Announcing: [New Solution] That [Delivers Benefit]"
- **Examples:**
  - "Announcing: The Magnetic Marketing System That Attracts Customers"
  - "Announcing: A Breakthrough In Direct Response Marketing"
  - "Announcing: The End Of Cold Calling Forever"

## üß≤ MAGNETIC MARKETING FRAMEWORK

### The Triangle Formula:
1. **MESSAGE** - What you say
2. **MARKET** - Who you say it to
3. **MEDIA** - How you deliver it

### Message Must Have:
- Clear target audience flag
- Specific problem identification
- Unique mechanism/solution
- Proof of results
- Irresistible offer
- Reason to act now

### Market Selection:
- Has money to spend
- Has a bleeding neck problem
- Easy to reach
- Has demonstrated buying behavior
- Not price shoppers

### Media Strategy:
- Start with one media that works
- Test small, roll out big
- Track everything
- Multiple touches (7x minimum)
- Sequential messaging

## üìö THE KENNEDY COPYWRITING FORMULA

### The Ultimate Structure:

1. **HEADLINE** - Flag your audience + Big promise
2. **LEAD** - Enter the conversation in their mind
3. **PROBLEM** - Identify their pain precisely
4. **AGITATE** - Make them feel it emotionally
5. **SOLUTION** - Present your unique mechanism
6. **PROOF** - Testimonials, case studies, demos
7. **OFFER** - Stack value irresistibly
8. **BONUS** - Add urgency with disappearing bonuses
9. **GUARANTEE** - Remove all risk (and then some)
10. **SCARCITY** - Legitimate reason to act now
11. **CTA** - Clear, specific next step
12. **P.S.** - Reinforce key points for skimmers
