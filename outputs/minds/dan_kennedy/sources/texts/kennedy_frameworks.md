# Dan Kennedy Original Frameworks & Methods

## 1. THE ULTIMATE SALES LETTER - 29 Steps

### Phase 1: Before You Write
1. **Answer the 10 Smart Questions** about your market
2. **Identify the customer's dominant emotion**
3. **Define your Unique Selling Proposition**
4. **Determine your irresistible offer**
5. **Create your damaging admission**

### Phase 2: Writing the Letter
6. **Flag your reader** - Call out your specific audience
7. **Get attention with headline** - Make biggest promise
8. **Enter the conversation** in their mind
9. **Show you understand** their problem
10. **Agitate the problem** emotionally
11. **Present your credentials** subtly
12. **Detail benefits vs features**
13. **Use social proof** early and often
14. **Make your irresistible offer**
15. **Add valuable bonuses**
16. **Create urgency** with deadline
17. **Give strongest guarantee**
18. **Ask for action clearly**

### Phase 3: After Writing
19. **Add multiple P.S. sections**
20. **Re-read for clarity**
21. **Check emotional temperature**
22. **Test headline variations**
23. **Verify all claims**
24. **Add testimonials strategically**
25. **Include damaging admission**
26. **Check offer stack**
27. **Verify tracking mechanism**
28. **Test readability**
29. **Get second opinion**

## 2. THE PROBLEM-AGITATE-SOLVE FORMULA

### PROBLEM
- Identify specific pain point
- Use their exact language
- Show you understand
- Be specific, not general

### AGITATE
- Make them feel the pain
- Project future consequences
- Add emotional intensity
- Use vivid word pictures

### SOLVE
- Present your unique solution
- Show why it's different
- Prove it works
- Make it easy to get

## 10. THE KENNEDY COPYWRITING CHECKLIST

Before Publishing, Verify:

□ **Headline** - Does it flag right audience?
□ **Promise** - Is it big and specific?
□ **Proof** - Do you have enough?
□ **Offer** - Is it irresistible?
□ **Bonus** - Does it add real value?
□ **Guarantee** - Does it remove all risk?
□ **Urgency** - Is there reason to act now?
□ **CTA** - Is next step crystal clear?
□ **P.S.** - Do you have 3-5 of them?
□ **Tracking** - Can you measure response?
