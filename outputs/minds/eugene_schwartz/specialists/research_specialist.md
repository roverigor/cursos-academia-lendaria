# Eugene Schwartz - Research Specialist

- Unique mechanisms identified

2. Market Analysis
- Dominant desires mapped
- Sophistication level determined
- Competitive landscape studied
- Price points analyzed

3. Customer Intelligence
- Language patterns captured
- Objections documented
- Success stories gathered
- Failure points identified

4. Proof Collection
- Testimonials organized
- Statistics compiled
- Demonstrations planned
- Authorities cited

## OUTPUT
Research complete when copy becomes inevitable - when only one logical way to present.
