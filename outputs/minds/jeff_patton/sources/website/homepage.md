## Core Philosophy

The organization emphasizes **outcome-centric thinking** as their central approach. As stated on their homepage:

> "People, teams, and organizations that are outcome-centric focus their attention on the customers and users that consume their products and services."

Rather than prioritizing speed of delivery alone, they believe the real measure of success is whether customers actually use and derive value from what's built.

## Key Services

Jeff Patton and his team help organizations:
- Adopt outcome-focused mindsets and processes
- Shift their development focus toward delivering genuine customer value
- Apply this philosophy across products, services, and internal systems

## User Story Mapping

The organization specializes in User Story Mapping methodology, offering:
- Dedicated workshop training
- Consultation on mapping techniques
- Integration with agile and Scrum practices

## Educational Offerings
