## Company Overview

Jeff Patton & Associates helps organizations build a culture and process focused on creating exceptional technology products. The firm blends Agile thinking, Lean methodologies, UX Design, Design Thinking, and Product Management into a comprehensive, product-centric approach to work.

## Services & Approach

The firm provides training and support to organizations seeking to improve their operational practices, partnering with clients across the US, Europe, Asia, and Australia.

## About Jeff Patton

**Background**: Jeff Patton founded the organization after decades in software development. In the late 1990s and 2000s, he served as a product leader at a company that became Salesforce Commerce Cloud.

**Expertise**: Since the early 2000s, he has been deeply involved in teaching and speaking within Agile, User Experience, and Product Management communities.

**Notable Work**: Patton authored the bestselling O'Reilly book *User Story Mapping*, which describes a holistic approach to using stories in Agile development while maintaining visibility of the broader context. The book is recommended for product managers, designers, and Agile practitioners.
