## Key Subjects Covered

The book addresses:
- **Agile software development** - Methodology and practices
- **User-centered system design** - Focusing on user needs and behaviors
- **Human-computer interaction** - Interface and experience design
- **Software development management** - Planning and execution strategies

## Book Philosophy

From the description, the book examines:
- How story mapping helps teams stay focused on users and their needs
- How changeable story maps enable better conversations throughout development
- High-level view of story mapping methodology
- How stories work in Agile and Lean projects
- The lifecycle of a story from conception to delivery
- Preparing stories during development for maximum effectiveness
