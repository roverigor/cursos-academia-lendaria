# Naval Ravikant - Periscope Session: June 6, 2018
**Topic:** Wealth Creation & Tweetstorm Discussion
**Source:** Podcast Notes compilation
**Duration:** ~1 hour
**Value Score:** 9/10

## Overview
Naval discusses his viral tweetstorm on wealth creation, covering fundamental principles of building wealth and creating value in society.

## Key Themes

### 1. Wealth Creation
- "Everyone in the world can be happy, and everyone in the world can be wealthy"
- Wealth is a positive-sum game, not zero-sum
- Focus on owning equity, not just renting out your time
- Making money solves external problems but doesn't guarantee happiness

### 2. Productizing Yourself
- Create a product from your unique skills
- Find what you do naturally and effortlessly
- Compete in areas where you can be uniquely valuable
- Be impatient with things that waste your time

### 3. Decision Making Principles
- When in doubt, the answer is "no"
- Choose the more difficult, painful path in the short term
- Make choices that provide internal peace
- "Hard choices, easy life. Easy choices, hard life."

### 4. Foundational Skills
- Learn persuasion
- Understand basic mathematics and accounting
- Develop specific knowledge unique to you
- Understand that failure is necessary for improvement

### 5. Happiness and Success
- Happiness is a skill you can develop
- "The first step to being happier, is to believe you can be happier"
- Value your time, it's all you have
- Internal peace is the ultimate metric

## Notable Quotes
- "Hard choices, easy life. Easy choices, hard life."
- "Value your time, it's all you have"
- "99% of all effort is wasted"
- "Everyone in the world can be happy, and everyone in the world can be wealthy"

## Key Advice
- Invest deeply in the right relationships and opportunities
- Be impatient with things that waste your time
- Understand that failure is necessary for improvement
- Focus on creating value, not just extracting it
