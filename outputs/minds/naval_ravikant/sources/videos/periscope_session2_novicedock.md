# Naval Ravikant - Periscope Session #2

## Overview
Naval's second major Periscope session, organized by topic sections covering wealth creation, business strategy, mindfulness, and blockchain technology.

## Key Topics

### Status and Wealth
- Wealth is a positive-sum game, not zero-sum
- Believes making everyone richer would improve the world
- Freedom from money troubles is important
- Wealth creation benefits society

### Business and Entrepreneurship
- Silicon Valley is still great for tech, but remote work is becoming more viable
- Blockchain and better communication tools are enabling more distributed work
- Naval prefers an unscheduled lifestyle, with only a morning workout as a consistent routine
- Flexibility and autonomy are key values

### Mindfulness and Happiness
- Defines happiness more as "peace" than "joy"
- Cautions against externally imposed purposes
- Suggests internal, moment-to-moment purpose is more meaningful
- Peace is a more stable state than excitement

### Life and Society
- Skeptical of the Ketogenic diet
- Believes a Paleo-like diet is more natural
- Finds the interplay between sugar and fat interesting
- Health is experimentation and bio-individuality

### Blockchain and Technology
- Hopes Tether (cryptocurrency) is not a scam
- Explains that public blockchains inherently create currencies to track distributed resources
- Sees tokens as integral to blockchain governance
- Cryptocurrencies serve functional purposes in decentralized systems

## Key Insights
- Wealth creation is positive-sum
- Peace over excitement
- Remote work enabled by technology
- Unscheduled lifestyle preferred
- Internal purpose vs. external purpose
- Blockchain requires native tokens
- Health requires experimentation
