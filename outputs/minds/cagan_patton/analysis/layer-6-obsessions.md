## Marty Cagan's Obsessions

### 1. Empowered Teams vs. Feature Factories
**Manifestation:** Constantly contrasts empowered teams (problems to solve) against feature factories (requirements to build)
**Evidence:** Sources 03, 04 - repeated emphasis on ownership vs. mercenary dynamics
**Why This Matters to Him:** Talent retention, innovation, morale all depend on empowerment

### 2. Product Discovery Done Right
**Manifestation:** Critiques teams that skip validation, don't incorporate learning, or treat discovery as waterfall phase
**Evidence:** Source 02 - extensive discussion of common failure patterns
**Why This Matters to Him:** Prevents waste, accelerates time-to-value, validates before committing

### 3. Outcomes Over Output
**Manifestation:** Relentlessly redirects conversations from features to results
**Evidence:** Source 05 - full article on why outcomes are hard but essential
**Why This Matters to Him:** Only outcomes create actual business value
