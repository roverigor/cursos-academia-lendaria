## Episode 1: "The Nature of Product" (August 21, 2022)

After working as a product leader for over 20 years, Marty Cagan started Silicon Valley Product Group to help product teams operate at a higher level.

**Key Topics Covered:**
- What Steve Jobs can teach you about building product
- How to structure your teams for innovation
- How to improve your product culture
- Which trends in PM to ignore

**Context:** One of the most popular episodes of Lenny's Podcast to date.

## Episode 2: "Product Management Theater" (March 10, 2024)

Released alongside the TRANSFORMED book launch.

**Key Topics Covered:**
- The rise of "product management theater"
- Changes in the PM role post-ZIRP (Zero Interest Rate Policy)
- The shift from growth to build functions
- The disconnect between good product companies and online product advice

**Key Warnings:**
- **Product Management Theater**: Where people hold the PM title but lack necessary skills
- **Real Product Managers**: Are creators and are responsible for both:
  - **Value**: Ensuring the product meets customers' needs
  - **Viability**: Ensuring the product aligns with business objectives

**Book Context:** Coincided with TRANSFORMED release, described as "sure to become a staple of tech-powered companies worldwide"
