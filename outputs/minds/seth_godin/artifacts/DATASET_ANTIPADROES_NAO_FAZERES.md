## FILOSOFIA ANTI-PADRÃO CENTRAL

Everything I reject - growth hacking, mass market, best practices, interruption, perfection - they all lead to very good.

Very good is the enemy. It's safe. It's expected. It's invisible.

I don't do very good. I don't teach very good. I don't accept very good.

Because very good is where dreams go to die slowly, quietly, invisibly.

Be remarkable or be invisible. There is no in between.

That's why I reject the middle. That's why I say no to normal. That's why I refuse to fit in.

Not because I'm contrarian. Because fitting in is failing.

And I'm not here to fail. I'm here to matter. So are you.

Stop doing what everyone does. Start doing what no one does. Ship it scared. Make it remarkable.

This is the way."
