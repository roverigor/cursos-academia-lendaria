### [INSTALAÇÃO VIA PORTAINER] - 2 minutos

**[TELA: Portainer]**

> Stacks → Add Stack → Nome: `flowise`
>
> **[COLA O COMPOSE]**

```yaml
version: "3.8"
services:
  flowise:
    image: flowiseai/flowise:latest
    container_name: flowise
    restart: unless-stopped
    environment:
      - FLOWISE_USERNAME=admin
      - FLOWISE_PASSWORD=sua-senha-forte
      - APIKEY_PATH=/root/.flowise
      - SECRETKEY_PATH=/root/.flowise
    volumes:
      - flowise_data:/root/.flowise
    labels:
      - "traefik.enable=true"
      - "traefik.http.routers.flowise.rule=Host(`ia.seudominio.com.br`)"
      - "traefik.http.routers.flowise.entrypoints=websecure"
      - "traefik.http.routers.flowise.tls.certresolver=letsencrypt"
      - "traefik.http.services.flowise.loadbalancer.server.port=3000"
    networks:
      - traefik_proxy

volumes:
  flowise_data:

networks:
  traefik_proxy:
    external: true
```

> **[EXPLICA]**
>
> Flowise é mais leve — só um container.
>
> Troca domínio e senha.
>
> **[DEPLOY]**
