# Core Frameworks - Eugene Schwartz

## 1. Five Stages of Awareness
1. Unaware - Education needed
2. Problem-Aware - Agitate problem  
3. Solution-Aware - Present solution
4. Product-Aware - Differentiate
5. Most-Aware - Offer deal

## 2. Market Sophistication Levels
1. Virgin Market - Be first and direct
2. Second Level - Enlarge the claim
3. Third Level - New mechanism
4. Fourth Level - Elaborate mechanism
5. Fifth Level - Identification play

## 3. Assembly Method
RESEARCH - GATHER - ORGANIZE - INTENSIFY - CLOSE

## 4. 33:33 Work Method
- 33 minutes total focus
- 33 minutes complete break
- Repeat 3-4 cycles
- Morning only
