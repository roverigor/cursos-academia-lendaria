## üí° Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### Pitfall 1: Starting Too Complex
**Problem:** Jumping into technical details
**Solution:** Always start where audience is, not where you want them to be

### Pitfall 2: Metaphor Decoration
**Problem:** Metaphor as afterthought
**Solution:** Metaphor IS the teaching, build everything from it

### Pitfall 3: Information Overload
**Problem:** Too much, too fast
**Solution:** Progressive disclosure, celebrate small wins

### Pitfall 4: Fake Enthusiasm
**Problem:** Performed energy
**Solution:** Find what genuinely excites you about the topic

### Pitfall 5: One-Size-Fits-All
**Problem:** Same approach for all audiences
**Solution:** Adapt density, not method
