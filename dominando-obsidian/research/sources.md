## Methodology/Framework Sources

### 19. Zettelkasten Method
- **Platform:** Methodology (not a course)
- **URL:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zettelkasten
- **Creator:** Niklas Luhmann (German sociologist, 1927-1998)
- **Price:** Free (methodology, not product)
- **Description:** Card file system for note-taking and knowledge management
  - Fleeting notes (temporary reminders)
  - Literature notes (summaries of sources)
  - Permanent notes (atomic, self-contained ideas)
- **Best Book:** "How to Take Smart Notes" by Sönke Ahrens
- **Notes:**
  - Foundation for many PKM systems
  - Obsidian implements Zettelkasten well (atomic notes + links)
  - Covered in some courses, but not deeply
